Goals

by Dumziii Mofolo on 02 March 2024

This blog post is about my user alignment goals and the intention of this site. It also delves into my wireframes ideas.

User alignment as a concept largely encompasses the way the layout, the content, and features of a website meet and are in line with the needs and expectations of the users that will interact and engage with your website. With that said, more than anything, I want my website to showcase and offer a lot more than I can do academically. I want users to see beyond my academic capabilities and to experience my thoughts especially my commentary on socio-cultural, political, and current affairs that affect myself as well as the users that would potentially be interacting with the website. Exploration of my academic skillsets remains the key however its not the only purpose the existence of my website will serve.

Target Audience

Future Employers = a fixed portion of my website will serve to be an extension of my academic skillset from which I would hope for employers to derive meaning from.
Young, inquisitive people interested in discourse around socio-cultural issues that affect our livelihoods especially within a South African context.

Type of Content

With this website, I aim to create a balance of showcasing my academic skill set and capabilities with my own reviews and analyses of socio-cultural and political issues affecting SA citizens within a South African context. The commentary within this website will also extend to game development and subject matters that encompasses the function that games aim to serve within the demographic that interacts with them.

User Experience

To create this balance and hopefully maintain it, this website will be divided into sections that detail and showcase my academic skill set as well as expose users to thoughts around game studies and existing artefacts. These thoughts will also involve my social commentary and thus a distinction in the form of ‚Äúheaders‚Äù will become very useful in guiding users exploring the website, knowing where to go from which point to reach the next point. The point of all this is to induce an experience of what an extension of me would feel like within the constraint of a website. Users engaging with these various aspects of the website will hopefully experience the well rounded balance i speak about and will probably have a tool from which they can examine and critique all information that was presented to them.

Interface Elements

To foster an easy-to-understand directory and a consistent guidance throughout the website at large, the following are elements I aim to use: Input Controls = The use of buttons and text fields. These will also be color coded intuitively to propel users to know that they should be clicking a specific button at a specific point in time within the stage or level of the website they would be in. Navigation Controls = The use of a search field right at the top of each webpage in the website that will aid the user in finding or looking up something they may think about while on the website or to accelerate the exploring of the website faster. Informational Controls= The use of tool tips could aid the users in navigating the website a bit more efficiently.
